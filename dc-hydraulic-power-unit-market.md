<p><p>The DC Hydraulic Power Unit market is primarily segmented into two types: Single Acting and Double Acting units. Single Acting DC Hydraulic Power Units use hydraulic pressure to drive a load in one direction, relying on gravity or a spring return, making them simpler and often less expensive. In contrast, Double Acting DC Hydraulic Power Units can exert force in both directions, offering greater control and efficiency for applications requiring precise movement and the ability to handle heavier loads.</p></p>
