<p><p>The seat belt market is categorized into several types based on design and safety features. Two-point safety belts secure the occupant at the lap, typically found in older vehicles. Shoulder belts add upper body restraint for enhanced protection. Three-point safety belts combine both lap and shoulder restraints, offering the most common and effective safety type in modern cars. Four-point safety belts provide additional security by anchoring at four points, often used in racing and specialized vehicles for maximum protection during collisions.</p></p>
