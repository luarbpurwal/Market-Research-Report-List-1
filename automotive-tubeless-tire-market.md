<p><strong>The Automotive Tubeless Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial</li><li>Bias</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive tubeless tire market comprises two main types: radial and bias tires. Radial tires have fibers arranged in a radial pattern, allowing for better heat dissipation, improved fuel efficiency, and enhanced traction, making them ideal for modern vehicles. In contrast, bias tires feature crisscross layers, providing a sturdier construction suitable for off-road and heavy-duty applications. While radial tires dominate the passenger car segment due to their performance attributes, bias tires continue to serve niche markets like agricultural and industrial vehicles.</p></p>
