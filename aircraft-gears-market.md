<p><p>The aircraft gears market consists of various types, including spur gears, bevel gears, helical gears, and others. Spur gears are used for parallel shaft applications, offering simplicity and efficiency. Bevel gears, featuring conical shapes, enable the transmission of power between intersecting shafts. Helical gears, with their angled teeth, provide smoother engagement and reduced noise, making them preferred for high-speed applications. Other gear types encompass worm gears and planetary gears, contributing to diverse functionalities in aircraft systems and enhancing overall performance and reliability.</p></p>
