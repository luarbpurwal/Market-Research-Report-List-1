<p><strong>The PEEK Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extruded PEEK Sheets</li><li>Calendered PEEK Sheets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PEEK sheet market is primarily divided into two types: extruded and calendered PEEK sheets. Extruded PEEK sheets are produced by forcing molten material through a die, resulting in uniform thickness and excellent mechanical properties, making them ideal for demanding applications. In contrast, calendered PEEK sheets are created by rolling the material between heated rolls, allowing for thinner sheets and enhanced surface finishes. Both types cater to industries requiring high-performance materials, such as aerospace, automotive, and medical applications.</p></p>
