<p><ul><li>Needle Coke</li><li>Shot Coke</li><li>Sponge Coke</li><li>Honeycomb Coke</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calcined petcoke market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **Needle Coke** is characterized by its high conductivity and low expansion, making it ideal for electrodes in electric arc furnaces.</p><p>2. **Shot Coke** features irregular, spherical shapes and is often used in metallurgical applications, providing good thermal stability.</p><p>3. **Sponge Coke** has a porous structure, enhancing its usability in batteries and as a carbon source.</p><p>4. **Honeycomb Coke** offers structural strength and is typically used in petrochemical industries for its thermal and chemical properties.</p></p>
