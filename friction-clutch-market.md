<p><strong>The Friction Clutch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Disc Clutch</li><li>Double Disc Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The friction clutch market is primarily categorized into single disc and double disc types. A single disc clutch features one friction plate, commonly used in lightweight vehicles for efficient torque transmission and simplicity. In contrast, a double disc clutch incorporates two friction plates, providing enhanced torque capacity and smoother engagement, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications like trucks and performance vehicles. Both types aim to optimize performance and reliability in various automotive and industrial applications, catering to diverse needs.</p></p>
